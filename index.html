Hey, I'm Sheikh Sohail. Although I don't come from a Computer Science background—I'm actually from the medical field—my passion for technology led me to explore coding as a creative outlet, much like painting or playing the guitar, which are also my hobbies.

My journey into tech began back in my 12th grade. Over time, what started as curiosity has grown into a deep passion for web development. I'm especially drawn to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, Python and I find myself most excited when working on frontend design. Outside of coding, I love diving into new technologies, experimenting with fresh ideas, and continuously learning to stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech world. I also enjoy contributing to the developer community, whether it's through open-source projects or sharing insights that might help someone else grow. Every line of code I write brings me closer to my vision of building meaningful and innovative digital experiences.
